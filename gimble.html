CollectMore : A Mobile Application

About The App

CollectMore gives you advice based on your input, the pupose of the app is to help you collect your debt. Based on the amount of debt, the time it remains outstanding, what the client has told you and other information, the app would use internal logic to identify which advice suits you best.
In addition to that the app also gives you SMS and Email templates that you can use to contact your client and it even recommends one for each of your unique debts.
