A case study on how it was built and how much did it cost."][vc_column_text]It all started with a simple request: [/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]

About the app: CollectMore gives you advice based on your input, the purpose of the app is to help you collect your debt. Based on the amount of debt, the time it remains outstanding, what the client has told you and other information, the app would use internal logic to identify which advice suits you best. In addition to that, the app also gives you SMS and Email templates that you can use to contact your client and it even recommends one for each of your unique debts. Paul's app is now one of the

top 50 downloaded business apps in Australia[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]

How is it built? It’s an offline native application built in Android & Swift with an internal SQLite database and a large number of conditional statements. It’s quite simple and straightforward and Paul was helpful by providing documentation that explained if which input is equal to what then which template is the output, which made the development simple.[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]

How much did it cost? In January 2016 Paul approached me after having built iOS version of the application for $11,000. I helped him build an Android version through an overseas developer for $2000, with an $9000 difference Paul got the same application built in Android. After a year of success, Paul approached me again wanting to build a second version of the app for both platforms, and they both cost $4000 and that’s including my consulting fee. The interesting thing about the cost of this application is that there’s little management required due to the simplicity of the app, and since we already have all the user interface documented there was a small room for any error to occur, that made me wonder why it would cost $11,000 in the first place for a single
